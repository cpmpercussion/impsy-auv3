# App Store screenshots

Submission-ready screenshots, generated by `scripts/capture-screenshots.sh`
(which drives `ScreenshotCaptureTests`). Light and dark variants of all three
screens — Dashboard, Settings, Mapping.

| Folder | Size | App Store slot |
|--------|------|----------------|
| `iphone-6.9/` | 1320 × 2868 | iPhone 6.9" display (primary iOS requirement) |
| `ipad-13/`    | 2064 × 2752 | iPad 13" display (required for iPad) |
| `macos/`      | 2880 × 1800 | Mac (16:10; the IMPSY window composited on a branded canvas) |

iPhone and iPad shots are full-screen with a mocked 9:41 status bar. macOS
shots are the app window composited onto a 16:10 canvas (cream for light,
deep green for dark) to satisfy the Mac App Store's 16:10 requirement.

Regenerate:

```bash
./scripts/capture-screenshots.sh all
# outputs to /tmp/impsy-screenshots/{iphone,ipad,macos,macos-appstore}
```

Upload at most 10 per device class in App Store Connect. Pick light or dark
(or mix) per your listing's look.
